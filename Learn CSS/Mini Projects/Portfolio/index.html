About Me

Hello! I'm Raj Bhushan Dixit, a Full Stack Developer and a recent graduate with a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Ramaiah Institute of Technology. My journey in computer science began in 2018, driven by a deep fascination with how technology and the internet can tangibly improve our lives. I'm passionate about building efficient, scalable solutions that make a real-world contribution. My primary expertise is in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js), and I thrive on building end-to-end applications, from designing a responsive React component to engineering the back-end API and database logic that powers it.

Through a series of internships at companies like GrapplTech, CodTech IT Solutions, and VirtuNexa, I translated complex requirements into clean, functional code and sharpened my skills in frontend development and remote collaboration. While my focus is the JavaScript ecosystem (React, Node.js, Express.js, JavaScript ES6+), my academic foundation also gave me a strong command of Java, Python, C/C++, SQL, and PHP, allowing me to approach challenges with a versatile mindset. I'm also proficient with modern frontend tools like Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, Git, and GitHub.

I am actively seeking new opportunities where I can apply my skills as a Full Stack Developer to a challenging and collaborative team. I'm eager to contribute, grow, and help build the next wave of innovative technology. If my profile sounds like a good fit, I'd love to connect.
